Project Idea

Overview

Designed for IOS and Android, the application will provide up to date information on your location sourcing existing sites, governmental and commercial. The concept of a one stop shop for all relevant topics will be housed in a neat compact frame. The option for local businesses to provide offers and or specific services will assist in funding hosting and other costs on an ongoing basis.

Motivation

What excites us is the potential for bringing together the community in a modern way by providing the most important local feeds in one easy to use application. In addition, the potential for growth financially through sponsorship deals and advertising offers investors a solid return on their stake. Promotion of the new application can be conducted locally with easy-to-use instructions on how to run this on customers’ phones. Being a free application ensures a high level take up and data of the user base will be a useful selling point to sponsors and advertisers.

Description

Although the base services of the application, as described above, are minimal, these can be tailored to suit each location or zone in larger cities. The dashboard will host this service and will be sourced from relevant governmental, council or commercial services. Some of these may incur fees for the provider. From the dashboard, users can access more detailed information they may be interested in and feature a particular offer, event or emergency. Notifications will also feature this aspect of the application. Users will be encouraged to have the application open throughout updating the information on a timely basis. The concept that users need to have this on their phones to keep in touch with all things local is to be at the forefront of marketing the product

Tools and Technologies

A relatively simple application to design for mobile technology, ‘keeping it local’ combines existing data and presents it in a simple and easy to use format. Its footprint will be relatively small as it will not host the majority of the data, instead it will stream it into the dashboard. Logos and advertising will need to be hosted but these are quite small in size. Maintenance will be relatively simple but ensuring all live links are kept functioning and active is essential. Users will be able to select what is featured on their dashboard, but sponsors and advertisements will not be user selectable. Simple icons will be used to signify the weather or alerts which can be used to call further information.

Skills Required

The development team will be small and the expectation of a rapid development cycle and testing would see the application available in a short period of time. Marketing can commence during this process to build demand and excitement. The size of the resulting program must be as small as possible and not demand any intensity in terms of battery life or memory allocation. It should be created to run in the background taking minimal resources. If this is not achievable the product will be deleted as users notice a drain or slowness in their mobile device. Staffing the development is therefore key to its success as a viable product.

Outcome

It is proposed the first two location be small villages or suburbs where we can ascertain quickly what functions well and what is most relevant to people. It will give us the data to use in later rollouts. It is hoped that success in carefully chosen locations will create interest and a desire for something similar in other places. Various marketing techniques will be used to encourage participation especially for businesses in the area. What it will bring to phone technology is a suite of useful data in one place relevant to the users place of residence and support local communities in. varying ways. There is not at this time any application that is this specific, most refer to much larger areas and are not personalised in this manner. We hope that with suitable technical skills, design attributes and a modest investment to launch this product within a short time period of three to six months. In the retime we are seeking suitable start up locations.
